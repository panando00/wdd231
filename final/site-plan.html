Math Lab Station Site Plan

Mini TEMU-style store for affordable learning resources for students in Africa

1. Site Name

Math Lab Station (domain example: mathlabstation.com)

2. Site Purpose

Provide a simple, mobile-first storefront where families and schools can purchase affordable educational products such as stationery, phonics cards, children’s storybooks, art supplies, and study resources. The site highlights promotions, supports product filtering, and offers clear contact and ordering information.

3. User Scenarios

  • A parent wants low-cost phonics cards and a starter set of crayons and searches products by category and price.
  • A teacher needs to bulk order exercise books and checks stock, unit pricing, and delivery options.
  • A guardian views featured storybooks, opens a details modal, and adds items to a shortlist remembered on the device.

4. Color Schema

Teal — #0ea5a6
Primary brand for headings, links, buttons, accents
Amber — #f59e0b
Secondary accent for badges, highlights, sale chips
Deep Gray — #1f2937
Body text and high-contrast elements
Soft Background — #f7f8fa
Page backgrounds and cards

Schema Applied in This Document
